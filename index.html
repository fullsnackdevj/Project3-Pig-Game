Pig Game Mechanics:

  • Objective: The first player to reach a total score of 50 wins the game.
  • Players: There are two players who take turns rolling a dice and accumulating points.
  • Rolling the Dice: Each player rolls a dice, and the number they roll is added to their current score.
  • Holding the Score: Players can hold their current score at any time during their turn by pressing the "Hold" button. The current score is added to their total score, and the turn switches to the next player.
  • Rolling a 1: If a player rolls a 1, and they havenâ€™t held their current score, their current score resets to 0, and they lose all the points accumulated during that turn.
  • Switching Turns: After pressing the Hold button or rolling a 1, the turn passes to the next player.

Strategy Tip: Be strategic! Hold your score before taking too many risks. If you roll a 1 without holding, your turn's progress resets to zero!

Game Flow:

  1. Player 1 rolls the dice, and the number is added to their current score.
  2. Player 1 decides to either roll again or hold their score.
  3. If Player 1 holds, the current score is added to the total, and it switches to Player 2.
  4. If Player 1 rolls a 1, their current score resets to 0, and Player 2 takes over.
  5. The game continues until one player reaches a total score of 50.
